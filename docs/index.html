The problem

Knowledge walks out the door every day.

Important context lives scattered across inboxes, chat threads, and people's memories — and disappears the moment someone's on vacation, changes teams, or leaves the company.

📥

Buried in email

Decisions and policies get made once, then forgotten in a thread no one can find again.

🙋

"Ask Sarah, she knows"

Institutional knowledge lives in a few people's heads instead of somewhere searchable.

🌀

New hires start from zero

Onboarding means re-explaining things that were already written down somewhere, once.

How it works

Three simple steps.

1 📤

Upload

A manager uploads a document — a policy, a contract, meeting notes, anything.

→
2 🧠

Second Brain reads it

It quietly reads, organizes, and summarizes the document in the background.

→
3 💬

Anyone can ask

Employees ask questions in plain English and get answers pulled straight from it.

Answers you can trust

It never makes things up.

Second Brain only answers from documents your company has actually uploaded. Every answer shows its sources and how confident it is — and if the answer isn't in there, it says so instead of guessing.

Who can do what

A clear hierarchy, so access is never a guessing game.

Every person is given exactly the level of access they need — and access granted higher up the org chart naturally applies to everything underneath it, so permissions never need to be set up twice.

Founder

Full control of the entire organization — every workspace, every setting, from day one.

Admin

Manages a workspace: creates sub-teams or client spaces, grants and revokes access, sets retention rules.

Manager

Uploads and manages documents for their team, plus everything a team member can do.

Team member

Asks questions and gets trustworthy, cited answers from everything they have access to.

Beyond search

It reads the fine print, so people don't have to.

The moment a document finishes uploading, Second Brain quietly writes a short summary and pulls out any concrete decisions it finds — so the gist is visible at a glance, without opening the file.

📝

Instant summaries

A plain-language summary appears automatically next to every document in the library.

📌

Key decisions surfaced

Concrete decisions buried in long documents are pulled out and listed separately.

Built for real companies

Compliance and governance, built in.

🗓️

Retention policies

Set how long documents should be kept — old ones are automatically cleaned up on schedule.

🔒

Legal holds

Freeze a document (or a whole workspace) so it can't be deleted while it's needed for a legal matter.

🧾

Full audit trail

Every sign-in, upload, access grant, and deletion is permanently logged — who, what, and when.

Privacy first

Your documents never leave your servers.

Second Brain is self-hosted by design. Files are stored on your own infrastructure — never a third-party cloud bucket — and the AI that reads them to build search runs locally too. The only outside services involved are Google (for signing in) and Anthropic's Claude (to have the conversation) — nothing else ever sees your data.

🏠

Self-hosted storage

Documents live on disk that your company controls, not a shared cloud service.

🔍

Local search engine

The technology that makes documents searchable runs entirely on your own servers.
